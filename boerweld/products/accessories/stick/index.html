Stick Welding Accessories

Stick Electrode Storage Can

Keeps electrodes fresh and dry with an airtight seal. Convenient belt clip and internal divider with four separate storage areas. Electrodes not included.

  • #770073

Electrode Storage Can Value Pack

Contains electrode storage can with 4-way divider and belt hook and the following electrodes: (1 lb.) 1/8"- 6011 , (1 lb.) 1/8"- 6013 ,(1 lb.) 1/8"-7018AC .

  • #770194

200 Amp Electrode Holder

200 amp, solid brass spring-loaded jaws accept rods up to 3/16 inch in 45, 90, or 180 degree positions.

  • #770026

300 Amp Electrode Holder

300 amp, solid brass spring-loaded jaws accept rods up to 1/4 inch in 45, 90, or 180 degree positions.

  • #770027

400 Amp Electrode Holder

Accepts rods up to 5/16 inch. A quarter turn of the handle loosens the rod for removal or replacement. Electrodes burn within 1 inch from the head and can be bent to any angle and still be held secure. Compact design accepts #2 cable.
