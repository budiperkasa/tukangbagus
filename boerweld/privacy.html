Hobart Welding Products Privacy Policy

Hobart Welding Products ("Hobart Welding Products") respects your personal privacy and is committed to adhering to all applicable U.S. and Canadian laws and industry guidelines relating to privacy and the protection of personal information. This privacy policy ("Privacy Policy") describes how we handle the personal information that you provide to us on this website ("Web Site"). This Privacy Policy applies only to the Hobart Welding Products Web Site, not to any other ITW or third party web sites that may be accessible from here. After reading this Privacy Policy, which is incorporated into and is a part of this Web Site's terms and conditions, you will know:

  1. What information Hobart Welding Products collects from you

  5. What Hobart Welding Products does about children visiting this Web Site

Please be sure to read this Privacy Policy before using this Web Site or submitting information to us. Our Privacy Policy may change from time to time and your continued use of this Web Site is acceptance of such changes, so please check this area periodically for updates to this Privacy Policy.

 

1. Information Hobart Welding Products Collects From You

Active Information Collection Like many other web sites, we actively collect information from our visitors both by asking specific questions on this Web Site and by permitting you to communicate directly with us via online forms and e-mail. Some of the information that you submit may be personally identifiable and/or personal information under U.S. or Canadian law. Such personally identifiable or personal information includes information that can be uniquely identified with you, such as your name, business name, address, phone number, e-mail address, etc. However, under Canadian law, "personal information" does not include your business address or other information used to contact you at your place of work. Contact information may be used to send information about our company to you and to learn about customer satisfaction with our services. We run contests on our Web Site in which we may ask you for contact information. The contact information may be used to contact you when necessary.

Passive Information Collection As you navigate through a web site, certain information can be passively collected (that is, gathered without you actively providing the information) using various technologies and means, such as navigational data collection. Like most web site operators, we track IP addresses during your visit and use both session and persistent cookies. We use cookies to help identify you when you visit our Web Site and to keep track of your shopping list. You should be aware that we might use these cookies for other purposes now and in the future. Our system also automatically gathers information about the areas you visit on this Web Site and collects operational information about the technology you use, such as your browser, type of computer, operating systems, and Internet service providers.

We passively collect information to generate statistics and measure site activity in order to understand what areas of this Web Site are most popular, which areas may require improvement and what technologies our visitors are using. This information helps us to update and improve this Web Site and enhance the usefulness of customer visits. We also use this information for security purposes, to detect and to block security breaches and to provide you with a safe online environment.

If you are a resident of Quebec, you are hereby informed that (1) the personal information collected by Hobart Welding Products would be considered a "file" being established by Hobart Welding Products within the meaning of applicable legislation of that province, (2) the "object" of that file is to use such personal information as described in Section 2 below, (3) the file will be created and kept by Hobart Welding Products and/or by one or more of its affiliates, and such file may be created and kept on its or their behalf by an outside service provider to Hobart Welding Products or such affiliate(s) at Appleton, WI (4) only those employees of Hobart Welding Products, such affiliate(s) and/or such service provider(s) whose job functions involve the fulfillment of the object of the file will have access to your file, and (5) if you wish to consult your file or make changes or corrections to it, you may do so by written request to Hobart Welding Products, PO Box 1079, Appleton, WI 54912.
