    hobart "roadshow" at TSC

    Saw the Hobart trailer yesterday. Tried the stickmate with some 7018AC and 7014 . The 7018ac were the cheapos and had the flux crumbling off. He got out the box of 3/32" 7014. They started easy and ran well on 1/8" flatbar . The stickmate was being powered by the Hobart elite. One 220volt machine or 2 120volt machines at a time. Or a plasma cutter and 120volt air compressor. I was the only one checking out the goods. Should have asked to give the Elite a try but it was behind the trailer, mostly to keep the noise level down and I had a restless kid pacing around.
