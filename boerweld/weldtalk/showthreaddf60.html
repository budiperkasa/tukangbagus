    The first thing I would do is replace the leads. I have picked up a few used welders over the years and the leads needed to be replaced. Take the stinger off and peal back the insulation a few inches, if the wire isn't nice and shiny they need to be replaced. I'm betting they are dark and black looking. Trust me the old welder will weld much better with new leads. I have been down this road before.

    Good luck and have fun!
