    Push the tip.

    pull off adapter

    The contact tip is screwed to an adapter, the adapter to the cable/hose. Pull it apart. You should have an inch of wire sticking out. Make sure the cable is straight and the wire should come out. If it does not, then change the liner. It takes the same liner as Miller M10/M15/M25. Just pick .035" liner. Do not cut a new liner short! It is supposed to stick out of the cable and into the back of the adapter. The liner has an O ring on it and takes some tugging. It comes out of the back end, not the gun end.

    When you run out of wire you need to cut that kinked up tag end off the wire before trying to pull the wire out though the tip end. Or cut the nub off the tip end and pull it back from the gun mounting end.

    Trying to pull that kinked up piece through that was tied off to the reel will break off inside the liner just about 98% of the time.

    Vicegrips advice was good for all things in general. Observe, ponder then act.
