    In Lanse's wire comparison video I notice he made no change to the machine settings. In my experience ( a good 20+ years on Lanse) for the same voltage setting, each one of those brands of wire requires slight different wire speed setting to get the arc dialed in as good as possible. Just trying to point out that L-56 may require a different wire speed dial setting then what you have previously been running.
