    anti-spatter spray

    Get the pressurized spray in the steel can. That makes the smoke and smaller spatter from fluxcore easier to get off. The "earth-friendly" type in a plastic spray bottle looks like watery milk and has lots of water in it. It seems to speed up rust and leaves a burnt residue on stuff. Probably easier on the lungs though.
