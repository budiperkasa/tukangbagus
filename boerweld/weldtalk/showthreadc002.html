    some difference

    I am impressed with the Hobart .030" fluxcore and think it may run a little cooler. Really, your "sweet spot" or best wire feed speed might be a little different. If you are welding 12g or thicker you probably should use the .035". I have ran Hobart, Lincoln, Blue Demon, and Anchor. Blue Demon fluxcore is made in USA but may be seconds or just made for a low price point. I have had a lot of porosity problems with it.
