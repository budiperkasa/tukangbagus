    Final Airplane

    It ended up not being a Bi Plane, but rather a wing-over. Fruit can engine, Truck Muffler placed atop a Golf Bag Cart. Used some 1/4" rod to frame out the fuselage and wing. Skinned with some scrap Mobile Home Skirting. Lawn Mower blade prop...'was going to shape and varnish a wood one...but this was simple and quick. Not operational at the moment.

    56 1/2" wingspan, length 60 " overall
