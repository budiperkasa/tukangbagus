    Black tape and superglue repair.

    I know that this is an old thread but I couldn't resist. When I got my former Lincoln 250/250 TIG Stick machine in 1995, it had two pin hole leaks it the liquid-cooled torch. IIRC, it was on the power line so that no connector could have been used. I took some black electrical tape and some superglue and wrapped both leaks with the glued tape. It fixed both leaks and it wasn't leaking when I traded it in ten years later. It worked for me.
