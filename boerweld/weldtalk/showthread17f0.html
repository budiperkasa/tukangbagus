    Always have questions. Question everything about what you are doing. The moment you get comfortable with how you are doing things is the moment you stop learning.even though I am the one that is training others, I still do my best to learn everything possible, even if it is not just about welding. Knowing materials that you are working on is key to success also. Something as simple as a mild steel can have something to learn about.
